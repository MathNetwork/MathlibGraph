\begin{abstract}
Formal mathematics libraries are strictly deterministic artifacts, yet they are built by humans through an uncertain process of exploration, organization, and judgment. We analyze the network structure of \module{Mathlib}—the largest unified library of formalized mathematics—and show that the deterministic product preserves measurable traces of the cognitive process that created it.
